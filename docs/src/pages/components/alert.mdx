# Alert

Use an alert to inform users of important information. To display an alert in a toast notification, use the [toast](/components/toast) module. Alert is modelled after the excellent version found in [Evergreen](https://evergreen.segment.com/components/alert).

#### Basic usage

```jsx
<Alert title="Nostrud non magna quis veniam dolore magna voluptate." />
```

#### With a subtitle

```jsx
<Alert
  title="Nostrud non magna quis veniam dolore magna voluptate."
  subtitle="Nulla id amet reprehenderit laboris irure Lorem ex esse eiusmod eiusmod occaecat officia. Quis in reprehenderit dolor veniam id sunt mollit reprehenderit."
/>
```

#### Intent

You can alter the colours and icons of the Alert by changing the `intent` prop to either `info`, `success`, `question`, `danger`, or `warning`.

```jsx
<div className="Alert-example">
  <Alert
    intent="info"
    title="Nostrud non magna quis veniam dolore magna voluptate."
    subtitle="Ut ut eiusmod ad cillum ad magna cillum non esse labore exercitation qui minim minim."
  />
  <Alert
    intent="success"
    title="Nostrud non magna quis veniam dolore magna voluptate."
    subtitle="Ut ut eiusmod ad cillum ad magna cillum non esse labore exercitation qui minim minim."
  />
  <Alert
    intent="question"
    title="Nostrud non magna quis veniam dolore magna voluptate."
    subtitle="Ut ut eiusmod ad cillum ad magna cillum non esse labore exercitation qui minim minim."
  />
  <Alert
    intent="danger"
    title="Nostrud non magna quis veniam dolore magna voluptate."
    subtitle="Ut ut eiusmod ad cillum ad magna cillum non esse labore exercitation qui minim minim."
  />
  <Alert
    intent="warning"
    title="Nostrud non magna quis veniam dolore magna voluptate."
    subtitle="Ut ut eiusmod ad cillum ad magna cillum non esse labore exercitation qui minim minim."
  />
</div>
```

#### Custom

You can provide your own components.

```jsx
<Alert>
  <Text variant="subtitle">
    Yeah, this is an absurd custom example, but I don't see myself doing this
    very often.
  </Text>
</Alert>
```
